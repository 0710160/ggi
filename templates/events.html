12 Days of Christmas - Community Treasure Hunt Information

12 Days of Christmas Greater Green Island Community treasure Hunt 10th 21st December

Between 10th and 21st December GGICN will hide 13 letters in 12 locations around the Greater Green Island community. Each day a clue will be revealed to lead you to a new location, with different letters. When the letters are unscrambled, the letters will create a secret Christmas phrase. Your job: Solve the clues, photograph the hidden letters, get the prize! Send your photos through to 12days@greatergreenisland.nz or message the GGICN Facebook page. Missed a day? No worries! Letters will remain in place the duration of the event so you can go back to earlier locations to hunt down the letters. Clues will be released in three places:

  • Greater Green Island Facebook page
  • Instagram @ggicn12days
  • https://greatergreenisland.nz/projects/list/19
The the three ways to win include:
  • 1. Be the first person to email or Facebook message us a photo of the letter at the most recently revealed location.
  • 2. Take a photo of all 13 letters, in the 12 locations and email them through to us.
  • 3. Unscramble the Christmas phrase and send that through to us for your chance at another spot prize.
Prizes have been kindly donated by:
  • Royal Albatross and Fort pass
  • Biggies pizza
  • Beachlands speedway tickets
  • Leap Dunedin entry
  • Olveston Historic Home tour
  • Dunedin Gasworks Museum tickets
  • Escape Dunedin

Greater Green Island Get Together 2020

A free community expo to celebrate our local clubs, groups and education providers. Register now.

Sign up to have an information/activity stall here: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeRQgx0PTAPuhhAiggK1wUXsS2uo5-0K3nCJwWSyN6Eo08TAQ/viewform?usp=sf_link This event is a celebration of all the clubs and groups in Abbotsford, Brighton, Burnside, Concord, Fairfield, Green Island, Waldronville, Ocean View and Westwood. No cost for not for profit groups/clubs and organisations. Please use this opportunity to showcase your group with a free activity, flyers and displays. The last Greater Green Island Community Get Together had around 2,500 people attending. Please note you will need to supply your own table - maximum size 2metres long please. We have plenty of chairs. The Greater Green Island Get Together will be held on Saturday 21 November, 12pm-3pm at the Dunedin City Baptist Church Building, 19 Main South Road, Concord. If you have any queries please contact Larna McCarthy on greatergreenislandcommunity@gmail.com.
