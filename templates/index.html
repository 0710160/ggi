About us

The Greater Green Island Community Network was established in 2013 when a group of like-minded people came together with a shared desire to foster a strong community which works together to develop its own solutions - a community where neighbourhoods are again full of people we know as our neighbours.

The Community Network has hosted a range of community events such as a teddy bear's picnic, astronomy evening, community expos and artisan markets, and has been instrumental in organizing road safety changes and community beautification.
